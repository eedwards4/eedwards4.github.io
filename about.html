I am a passionate programmer with a focus on cutting-edge technologies in the fields of LLMs (Large and Language Models), human-machine interfaces, and cybernetics. While I am still a student, I strive to learn more about how these technologies can be applied to improving our day-to-day lives.
 
My journey in programming began in high-school, when I got my first computer and was promptly blocked from much of what I wanted to access. I began to search for workarounds and eventually learned skills like batch scripting and the command line through my search. What I found in this initial foray into the world of computers piqued my interest, and I have been learning and growing ever since.
 
After taking a basic programming course, I realized that I really enjoyed programming and the things I can create with code, and decided to focus my life and career around developing those skills. I have always loved the idea of a personal AI, and discovered the concept of Machine Learning during my freshman year of college. I have since undertaken a few projects related to the subject (though you won't find those here as they are extremely simple). My dream is to build an AI assistant that personalizes itself to the user, learning everything from their schedule to their favorite color, and is able to assist with both daily tasks and complex problems.
 
I also take great interest in the field of cybernetics, as the possibility of enhancing myself through technology fascinates me. There are already many great applications of this technology out in the world helping people with disabilities, but as an athlete and a nerd I really want to see whether technology like synthetic muscle fibers is possible.
 
Beyond programming, I enjoy snowboarding, rowing, guitar, and flight simulators. Snowboarding and rowing bring out my athletic side, and continue the legacy I started as a child in swimming/diving. When I'm working out or competing I feel an extreme sense of calm and centeredness that nothing else can really bring. Guitar is a great creative outlet, and whether I'm writing my own music or learning riffs I feel a real emotional connection to the instrument. Flight simulators allow me to manage my adrenaline-junkie tendencies without any real danger, and I love the feeling of shooting down virtual opponents after a difficult dogfight.
 
When it comes to music, I enjoy many generates, but a major contender would definitely be the Electro-Swing genre. I love the fusion of vintage jazz/blues with modern electronic effects and mixing, and the music never fails to ignite my creative side. With services like spotify allowing for constant musical uptime I sometimes feel like my life has a soundtrack, and I love the feeling of starting something right when the beat drops or the solo hits.
 
Through this website I hope to show you a little bit of myself and display my achievements for the world to see. Join me on this wild ride as we explore the possibilities of a truly boundless world. Let's explore together!
